What is programming?

Writing human "readable" text that is transformed into a series of commands a computer can understand

Programs can range from a few lines to a few million

Some examples of programs we use everyday

  • Apps on our phones
  • The operating systems on our computers
  • Text editors and slide creation programs that helped me make this presentation

Computers are very literal

They will do exactly what you tell them, even if it results in...

We can help prevent angry computers by taking a writing step by step instructions on what we intend our programs to do

This planning technique is called pseudocode

It can help us quickly find any faults in our program's logic before the first line of code is written
